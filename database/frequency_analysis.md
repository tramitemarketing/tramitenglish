## 60-Day Schedule Weighting

Based on frequency analysis, topic priority order for the 60-day plan:

1. **Verb Tenses** (days 1–7) — foundation, present/past/future all forms
2. **Modals** (days 8–14) — must/should/could/might/would, modal perfects
3. **Conditionals & Wish** (days 15–21) — II, III, mixed; wish/if only
4. **Passive** (days 22–27) — all tenses, causative have/get
5. **Reported Speech** (days 28–33) — statements, questions, commands
6. **Gerund & Infinitive** (days 34–39) — patterns, change of meaning
7. **Word Formation** (days 40–46) — prefixes, suffixes, all patterns
8. **Collocations & Phrasal Verbs** (days 47–53) — high-frequency pairs
9. **Connectors & Prepositions** (days 54–60) — linking words, fixed phrases
